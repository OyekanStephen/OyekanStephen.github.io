MISSION uniendo mundos

The anticipated alien encounter might not align with our expectations when we ponder the question, "What kind of world will the aliens meet?" our enthusiasm may diminish as we consider the reality of an extraterrestrial beings on Earth. What kind of humanization will they encounter, and what will they think about the state of our planet? Are we truly prepared for this unprecedented encounter? The 'Mission Uniendo Mundos' represents one of the most significant undertakings in human history, seeking to bridge life on Earth with the recently discovered species on Cellular B. This book delves into diverse aspects of earth such as humanization, climates, politics, wars, and historical events.
